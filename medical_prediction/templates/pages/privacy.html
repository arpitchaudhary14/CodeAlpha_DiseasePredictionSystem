Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Today

1. Data Collection

At MediSense, we take your privacy seriously. We only collect the data necessary to provide you with accurate AI-driven medical insights. Your medical inputs are processed securely.

2. Data Storage & AI Processing

When you upload documents for analysis (like PDFs or images), we process them directly in memory and securely transmit them to our AI providers (e.g., Google Gemini). We do not permanently store your raw uploaded documents on our servers. We only store the resulting text analysis in your history.

3. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual prediction history items, clear all history, or permanently delete your entire account from the Profile settings at any time.
